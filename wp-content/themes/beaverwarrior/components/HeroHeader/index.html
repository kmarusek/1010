Some modifications

            
HeroHeader supports several qualifiers which allow adapting the
            header to any particular background image. These qualifiers allow
            you to indicate the background color, it's focal point, and how to
            align the title and paragraph content. You may alter any of:

            
    
                
  • The background color, which also changes
                the text color for maximum contrast.
    • 
                
  • The image focal point.
    • 
                
  • The height may be decreased to thin
                class height.
    • 
                
  • The content may be aligned
                to the bottom or top.
    • 
            

            
These options do not conflict and may be applied in any
            combination.
